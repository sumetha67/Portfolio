Solid waste refers to any discarded or unwanted material generated from human activities, including residential, industrial, and commercial sources. It encompasses a wide variety of items, such as household garbage, construction debris, industrial by-products, and agricultural waste. Effective management of solid waste is crucial to prevent environmental pollution, protect public health, and conserve natural resources. This involves processes such as waste reduction, recycling, composting, and proper disposal in landfills or incinerators. Governments, businesses, and individuals all play a role in minimizing waste generation.

Project 2

Online electricity billing is a convenient system that allows users to manage and pay their electricity bills digitally. It eliminates the need for physical visits to billing offices by providing access through websites or mobile apps. Users can view their current and past bills, check usage history, and make payments using various online methods like credit cards, debit cards, net banking, or digital wallets. This system ensures quick, secure, and efficient transactions, reducing paperwork and saving time. Additionally, many platforms send reminders and provide detailed consumption reports, promoting better energy management.
